# Sources and provenance

The initial TIKL-26 research snapshot used or referenced:

- Feit, Weir & Oulasvirta (2016), *How We Type*, CHI. DOI: 10.1145/2858036.2858233.
- Dhakal et al. (2018), *Observations on Typing from 136 Million Keystrokes*, CHI. DOI: 10.1145/3173574.3174220.
- OpenAI `tiktoken` public encoding definitions.
- EleutherAI GPT-NeoX tokenizer assets.
- Google SentencePiece.
- Salesforce WikiText.
- Microsoft MS MARCO queries.
- UCI SMS Spam Collection.
- AutoHotkey, Karabiner-Elements, X.Org XKB, keyd, and Kanata documentation.

This repository contains derived metrics and manifests, not raw source corpora. Contributors must verify the terms of every source before adding data, tokenizer files, or model assets.
