# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in TIKL a harassment-free experience for everyone. Positive behaviour includes empathy, respectful disagreement, constructive feedback, and responsibility for mistakes. Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, personal attacks, sexualised language, deliberate intimidation, and publishing private information without permission.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behaviour they consider inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Report concerns privately through the contact method published at tikl.my. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This code is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
